A selling point: 
* Should convey the product or service's core benefit / They should answer questions like "What does this do for me?" or "Why should I care about this?" 
* Should highlight the primary advantages, unique features, 
* Should appeal to both rational and emotional decision-making 
* Are words and phrases that emphasize guarantees, warranties, or quality assurances to build trust and confidence in the product. 
* Should encourage the audience to engage immediately. 
* Price points can be considered as selling points in some circumstances
