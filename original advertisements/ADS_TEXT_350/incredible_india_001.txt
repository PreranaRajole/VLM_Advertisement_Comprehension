EXPERIENCE THE FLAVOUR OF RAJASTHAN

The royal state of Rajasthan, in India is one of the most unique places to visit in the world, for its amazing cultural heritage, amazing fortresses, phenomenal landmarks, bright colors, its mouth-watering fiery dishes and mesmerising folk music and dance forms. Jaipur - the Pink City, Jodhpur - the Blue City, Jaisalmer - the Golden City, Udaipur - the Lake City, Bikaner - the Camel City, Pushkar - the Sacred City of India, Ajmer - the Holy City of Rajasthan, Mandawa - the City of Havelis, and so on are prime vacationers places in the state. All this and a lot more awaits for you to experience in the magical state of Rajasthan !!!
